On Sunday, November 6th, from my deck I drew the tarot card, The High Priestess.

The High Priestess is the guardian and ruler of the subconscious, being the teacher of secrets and sacred arts. She symbolizes spiritual enlightenment, wisdom, and inner knowledge letting you know that now is the time to be still and re-examine your inner self, and that the answers are within you.

I've been doing a lot of self questioning about the road my life is on, and more specifically what I want from it recently. I feel as if this card is reminding me to continue to reflect and question myself, even if the process is awkward or painful the rewards are worth it in the end.
