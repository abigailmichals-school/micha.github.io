On Sunday, September 26th, from my deck I drew the tarot card, Strength.

Strength, is a card that represents strength, power and determination. It is a reminder that our most powerful strength lies within us, and that with it we can overcome lifes obstacles. It also reminds us to tame our instincts and to think things through rather then just acting impulsively. The card typically appears when one is going through a battle, whether it be a innier or outter battle.

This week has been particularly stressful for me, with lots of sudden problems and issues popping up. This has in turn led to my mental health becoming unwell. This card reminds me that I am stronger then I remember, and capable of overcoming these unexpected challenges.
