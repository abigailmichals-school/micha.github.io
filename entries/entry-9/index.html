On Sunday, November 13th, from my deck I drew the tarot card, The Hanged Man.

The Hanged Man is a reminder that sometimes you have to put things on hold in order to take the next step. The Hanged Man is a reminder from the universe to take a step back before the universe forces you to. It calls for you to revaluate the path you've taken, and the patterns and habits you've formed. When the pause time comes, its important to pay attention to it and take the time to rest, before you are forced to.

Right now in my life I've been evaluating my past and present, questioning the road ive taken as I feel I've fallen into a rut. This card is a reminder that reflection and breaks are good, even if I feel stressed that I am not making any progress, overall this will benefit me.
